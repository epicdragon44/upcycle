Our Story

CycleUp! began at Kode With Klossy, uniting us over a shared passion for fashion's societal impact. We aim to promote awareness while keeping the joy that comes with expressing oneself through clothing. Being digital natives, we have an innate understanding of how trends circulate on social media, giving us a modern insight into tackling fast fashion. Our blend of tech expertise and fashion awareness equips us to make CycleUp! a transformative force in promoting sustainable fashion practices.
